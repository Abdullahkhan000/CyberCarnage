What is CyberCarnage? ›

CyberCarnage is a curated digital vault — a fast, game-focused database built for players who want sharp information and a sleek gaming UI. It integrates CyberCarnage AI for interactive assistance and Disqus for community discussion. The platform prioritizes usability, clarity, and minimal distractions.

How does CyberCarnage AI work? ›

AI responds to user queries with up to 4 messages per day. All interactions are temporarily logged for session management, performance improvement, and debugging. Users are advised not to share personal or sensitive information. AI is optimized for gaming-related topics such as game titles, platforms, genres, and release information.

How do Disqus comments work? ›

Disqus collects usernames, email addresses, and IPs to enable commenting. Users retain ownership of their comments and can manage or delete their data directly through the Disqus platform. CyberCarnage does not store Disqus comments permanently on its servers.

Can I request a new game to be added? ›

Yes. Submit missing titles through the provided request form. All requests are verified to ensure accuracy before being added to the database. This keeps the platform reliable and up-to-date for all users.

Can I request updates or corrections to existing games? ›

Absolutely. If you notice outdated or incorrect information, you can report it and verified updates will be implemented promptly to maintain database accuracy.

Is CyberCarnage mobile-friendly? ›

Fully responsive. Every card, cover, and stats panel adapts across devices — from mobile phones to desktops and ultrawide monitors — ensuring readability and smooth navigation.

Is there a rating or review system? ›

CyberCarnage provides quick-glance stats and community impressions, helping users make informed decisions without information overload. Ratings are summarized to enhance clarity.

What should I do if AI is not responding? ›

Check your internet connection and daily message limit. If issues persist, reload the page. Temporary server interruptions can also cause delays, but your session and history remain intact.

How is my privacy protected? ›

No personal information is required to explore games. CyberCarnage AI logs messages temporarily for functionality only. Disqus data is managed by Disqus under their privacy policies. We do not sell or share personal user data beyond necessary security or analytics purposes.

Who do I contact for further help? ›

For queries about CyberCarnage, AI usage, or Disqus comments, use the Contact page. Messages are forwarded securely to ensure prompt assistance.
