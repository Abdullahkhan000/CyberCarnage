FAQ

Frequently asked curiosities, decoded with a hint of digital glow.

What is CyberCarnage? ›

CyberCarnage is a curated digital vault — a clean, fast, game-focused database built for players who like their information sharp and their UI sharper.

What type of data do you store? ›

Only game-related content: titles, platforms, genres, release info, images, and other public details. No personal user data is collected or required to explore the site.

Can I request a game to be added? ›

Yes. If a title feels missing from the grid, you can request it — and it’ll be added to the vault once verified.

Can I request data updates or corrections? ›

Absolutely. If something looks outdated, inaccurate, or incomplete, you can request a fix and it’ll be updated swiftly.

Is CyberCarnage mobile-friendly? ›

Entirely. Every card, cover, and stat panel adapts smoothly across all screen sizes — from handhelds to ultrawides.

Who maintains CyberCarnage? ›

A single creator runs the code, the content, and the curation — keeping everything fast, functional, and honest.

Is there any rating or review system? ›

Yes — CyberCarnage provides quick-glance stats, community impressions, and essential info so you can make fast decisions without drowning in noise.
